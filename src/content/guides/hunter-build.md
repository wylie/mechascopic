## Overview

ARC Raiders mixes PvE machine threats with hostile player encounters, so your "best build" is usually the one that helps you extract reliably.
This path is built for early solo progression where consistency matters more than forcing fights.

As of March 2026, the skill tree branches into Survival, Mobility, and Conditioning. For beginners, prioritize consistency over aggression.

## Recommended Early Skill Focus

- **Survival first:** Improve looting pace and stealth-related value so you spend less time exposed while scavenging.
- **Mobility second:** Take movement and repositioning upgrades that help you disengage when fights become noisy.
- **Conditioning third:** Add stamina/strength utility once your route discipline is stable.

## Low-Risk Loadout Principles

- Run gear you can afford to lose; extraction games punish over-investment early.
- Bring tools that support escape and repositioning (for example deployables or movement options) instead of only raw damage.
- Keep inventory space for high-value materials needed for workshop crafting and upgrades.
- Avoid prolonged fights unless they directly protect your extraction path.

## Run Flow That Scales

1. Start with a clear objective (quest item, material type, or route check).
2. Loot quickly and rotate often; the longer you stay, the more likely you are to attract machines or other Raiders.
3. If combat noise spikes, break line of sight and reposition instead of forcing a duel while outnumbered.
4. Extract earlier with smaller wins when your bag is already profitable.
