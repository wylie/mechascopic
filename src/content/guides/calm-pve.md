## Overview

ARC Raiders rewards controlled decisions more than nonstop fighting.
Because the surface is shared with machines and other Raiders, panic usually turns one mistake into a full wipe.

Use this reset routine between and during runs.

## Pre-Run Reset (30 Seconds)

1. Set one objective only (quest step, crafting material, or clean extraction).
2. Choose a "leave now" threshold before you deploy (for example: one high-value item or two quest items).
3. Accept the risk budget for that run so gear loss does not tilt your next decision.

## In-Run Composure Rules

- **If audio gets chaotic, slow down:** noise attracts attention and escalates third-party risk.
- **If your objective is complete, rotate to extract:** greed causes most avoidable losses.
- **If you are pinched by multiple threats, rotate instead of re-peeking:** solo fights get worse the longer they last.
- **If a fight drags, disengage:** surviving and extracting beats "winning" one long fight.

## Post-Run Review (1 Minute)

- Ask what single decision most affected the result.
- Keep one adjustment for the next run; avoid changing your whole playstyle at once.
- Track consistency by extraction quality, not only kill count.
